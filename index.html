Established in 1909, Abisko National Park is a crown jewel of Sweden’s Norrbotten County. Nestled within the Arctic Circle near the Norwegian border, this park spans an impressive 77 square kilometers (30 square miles) in the stunning Abiskodalen valley.

Abisko offers a one-of-a-kind experience for nature enthusiasts and adventurers alike. Depending on the season, you can marvel at the northern lights dancing across the winter sky or witness the midnight sun in summer, where daylight stretches endlessly. During the polar nights, the park transforms into a mysterious realm of perpetual darkness.

Beyond its scenic beauty, Abisko plays a vital role in preserving the northern Nordic wilderness in its purest form. It also serves as a hub for scientific research, safeguarding its natural treasures for future generations to explore and appreciate.
